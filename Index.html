YOUR FRIENDLY NEIGHBORHOOD

SPIDER-MAN

Peter Benjamin Parker. Bitten by a radioactive spider at the age of fifteen, orphaned since childhood, raised by his Aunt May in Queens, New York. He didn't ask for the powers. He didn't ask for the burden. But when the man he could have stopped walked past him and pulled a trigger — everything changed. With great power, there must also come great responsibility. That single truth has driven him through heartbreak, sacrifice, and loss that would break anyone else. He has died. He has forgotten. He has been erased from the world's memory. And yet Peter Parker keeps getting up, keeps putting on the mask, keeps swinging back into the fight — not because the world will remember him, but because someone has to. He is not the most powerful hero in the Marvel Universe. He might just be the greatest.
